Crispy Chicken Nuggets Recipe

Hello all visitors! I'm here to reccomend you one of my favourite recipes for my favourite food. This recipe is sure to provide you with the crispiest, juiciest chicken nuggets you will ever eat. I hope you enjoy the final product, and let's get right into it!

The required ingredients for 8 yield of servings include:

  • Vegetable oil
    • Used for frying chicken
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 tablespoons of garlic salt
  • 3 tablespoons of ground black pepper
  • 4 large eggs
    • Should be beaten
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
    • Should be cut into small chunks

Here are the steps required to make this fantastic chicken recipe. Keep in mind the preperation and cooking time are 15 minutes each. Ensure you follow proper kitchen safety rules when making this recipe.

  1. Gather all of your ingredients needed. Heat 1 inch oil in a large skillet or a saucepan to around 175°C
  2. Stir together your flour, garlic salt and pepper in a bowl. Dip your cut up chicken into eggs, then press each piece in your flour mixture. Ensure you coat it well, and shake off excess mixture. Place the coated pieces in a plate.
  3. In batches, fry the chicken in hot oil until it is a nice golden brown colour and no longer pink in the center.
  4. Use an instant-read thermometer to check the chicken's temperature. It should be at least 74°C
